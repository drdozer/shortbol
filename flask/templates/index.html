What is ShortBOL?

ShortBOL is a shorthand language for SBOL that makes writing synthetic designs natural and easy. It is concise and easy to read/write and does not limit the functionality that SBOL already provides.

ShortBOL is developed and maintained by Turing Ate My Hamster LTD and Christopher P D Taylor in partnership with Newcastle University.
